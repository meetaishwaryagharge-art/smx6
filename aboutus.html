About SMX India

Technology With An Edge

Sewing Machine Exchange India (SMX India), established in 1970 by our founder Mr. Santosh Agarwal, is a premier importer, exporter, and supplier of industrial sewing machines based in Mumbai. Our machines are sourced from Japan, Taiwan, China, Europe, and the USA.

With over five decades of industry knowledge, we consistently deliver the best sewing solutions and a high-quality user experience. We provide end-to-end solutions for the garment industry — from Single Needle Lockstitch to Computerized Embroidery systems.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

1970
Founded by Mr. Santosh Agarwal
SMX India began operations in Mumbai as a specialist importer and distributor of industrial sewing machines.
1985
Juki Authorised Partnership
Became an authorised dealer for Juki — one of Japan's leading industrial sewing machine brands.
2000
Expanded to Computerised Embroidery
Extended product range to include computerised embroidery and multi-head systems for large garment factories.
2010
Silversun & Richpeace Onboarded
Added cutting-edge brands Silversun and Richpeace to cater to automation and CAD/CAM cutting solutions.
2024
Pan India Network — 40+ Brands
Today SMX India represents over 40 global brands and serves thousands of garment manufacturers across the country.
