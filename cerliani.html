About the Brand

Why Cerliani?

Cerliani (MCE) is one of Italy's most respected manufacturers of precision sewing machine hooks, bobbin cases, and related components. Founded in 1945, their products are trusted by industrial sewing machine makers and garment factories worldwide for uncompromising quality and stitch consistency.

SMX India is the authorised distributor for Cerliani products across India. Every hook and bobbin case is manufactured to exact tolerances, ensuring smooth operation, reduced downtime, and superior thread handling across all fabric types.
